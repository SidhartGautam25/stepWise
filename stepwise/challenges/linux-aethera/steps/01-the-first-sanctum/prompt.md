# Welcome to Aethera
You have entered the magical land of Aethera (the `root`). Your soul is bound to this dimension, but you are not alone—a **Guide Spirit** (`bash`) has appeared to assist you. 

The Guide Spirit understands powerful spells. Your first task is to forge a new building called a **Sanctum** (folder).

Use the `mkdir` spell to create a Sanctum named `house`.
